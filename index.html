The Secret Society of Scandinavian Dreamers

A whimsical online community for enthusiasts of Scandinavian culture, language, and lifestyle. Members immerse themselves in the Nordic way of life while sharing their dreams, from learning Swedish to building a cozy cabin in the fjords.

Activities

  • Troll Hunting
  • Brown Cheese Shooting
  • Rudolph's Red Nose Balloon Pop
  • Ice Sculpting Challenge
  • Rainbow Viking Night
  • Furniture Building Race
